Prospective Students

If you are interested in doing research with me, please take a look at my research interests to make sure they align with yours. Also, please take a look at my recent publications.

Once you have done so, please send me an email letting me know which projects you found most interesting or if you have a closely related project in mind. Please make sure to attach your CV and unofficial transcripts to the email.

For graduate students, I normally expect that you have taken at least one graduate level course with me, including a course on compilers and/or parallel computing. This will ensure that you will not have a high learning curve getting started on a project, which would ultimately delay your thesis.
