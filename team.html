Information for Prospective Researchers

If you are interested in doing research with me, please take a look at my recent publications to make sure that my research interests align with yours. Once you have done so, please send me an email letting me know which topics you found most interesting or if you have a closely related project in mind. Please make sure to attach your CV and unofficial transcripts to the email.

I normally expect that you have taken at least one advanced course with me – such as a course on parallel computing or compilers – and scored a high grade in this course. This will ensure that you will not have a high learning curve getting started on a project.
